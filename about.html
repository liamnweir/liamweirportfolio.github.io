My Focus

Hello! I’m a third-year senior-standing student at the University of California, Santa Cruz majoring in Cognitive Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence and Human–Computer Interaction, and minoring in Electronic Music.

I’m especially interested in building human-centered AI systems in electronic music that are intuitive, expressive, and grounded in how people actually perceive and think.
