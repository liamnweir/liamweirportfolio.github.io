About Me

I’m a third-year student at the University of California, Santa Cruz majoring in Cognitive Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence and Human–Computer Interaction, and minoring in Electronic Music.

My academic work combines cognitive psychology, experimental design, data analysis, and programming with creative practices in sound and music. I’m especially interested in building human-centered AI systems that are intuitive, expressive, and grounded in how people actually perceive and think.

I am actively seeking internships, research opportunities, and job roles across AI, HCI, data analysis, and creative technology. I’m excited to collaborate, learn quickly, and contribute meaningfully to real-world projects.
