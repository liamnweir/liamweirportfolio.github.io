Services

What I Offer

Every instrument is different. I begin by listening to your concerns and assessing what the piano actually needs.

01

Standard Piano Tuning

Careful adjustment of the strings to improve pitch, balance, clarity, and consistency across the keyboard.

  • Overall pitch assessment
  • Keyboard-wide adjustment
  • Final playing and listening check
02

Light Regulation

Attention to accessible playing components when keys feel sticky, uneven, slow, or uncomfortable.

  • Sticky or sluggish key assessment
  • Uneven response
  • Basic playability improvements
03

Piano Cleaning

Detail-oriented cleaning to reduce dust and debris and freshen the appearance of the instrument.

  • Exterior dusting and polishing
  • Cleaning around the keys
  • Accessible interior dust removal
