Piano Services

Practical care for the sound, feel, and appearance of your piano.

Every instrument is different. The appropriate service will depend on your piano's condition, maintenance history, and the specific concerns you would like addressed.

01

Standard Piano Tuning

Careful adjustment of the piano's strings to improve pitch, balance, clarity, and consistency across the keyboard.

  • Overall pitch assessment
  • Adjustment across the keyboard
  • Improved harmonic balance
  • Final playing and listening check
02

Regulation

Adjustment of accessible playing components to improve keys that are sticky, uneven, slow, or uncomfortable to play.

  • Sticky or sluggish key assessment
  • Uneven key response
  • Minor key alignment concerns
  • Basic playability improvements
03

Cleaning

Detail-oriented cleaning to reduce dust and debris, freshen the instrument's appearance, and help it look cared for.

  • Exterior dusting and polishing
  • Careful cleaning around keys
  • Dust removal in accessible areas
  • General cosmetic refresh
