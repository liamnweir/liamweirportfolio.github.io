About Liam

A lifelong pianist with a genuine passion for the instrument.

Piano has been part of my life from an early age. Over the years, I have played as a pianist and keyboardist in multiple bands and live concerts, giving me firsthand experience with how important a responsive, balanced, and well-tuned instrument can be.

My interest eventually expanded beyond playing. I became fascinated by the piano itself—how it produces sound, how small adjustments affect the playing experience, and how careful tuning can restore clarity and musicality. Working on pianos became a natural continuation of my life as a musician.

Through my electronic music studies at UC Santa Cruz, I developed an even deeper understanding of pitch, timbre, acoustics, synthesis, critical listening, and music production. That education continues to influence both my creative work and the attention I bring to piano service.

Piano Services

Practical care for the sound, feel, and appearance of your piano.

Every instrument is different. The appropriate service will depend on your piano's condition, maintenance history, and the specific concerns you would like addressed.

01

Standard Piano Tuning

Careful adjustment of the piano's strings to improve pitch, balance, clarity, and consistency across the keyboard.

  • Overall pitch assessment
  • Adjustment across the keyboard
  • Improved harmonic balance
  • Final playing and listening check
02

Regulation

Adjustment of accessible playing components to improve the feel and responsiveness of keys that are sticky, uneven, slow, or otherwise uncomfortable to play.

  • Sticky or sluggish key assessment
  • Uneven key response
  • Minor key alignment concerns
  • Basic playability improvements
03

Cleaning

Detail-oriented cleaning to reduce dust and debris, freshen the appearance of the instrument, and help your piano look properly cared for.

  • Exterior dusting and polishing
  • Careful cleaning around keys
  • Dust removal beneath accessible strings
  • General cosmetic refresh
