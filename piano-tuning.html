Services

Thoughtful service for your instrument.

The correct service depends on the condition of the piano, how long it has been since its last tuning, and how the instrument will be used.

01

Standard Piano Tuning

A tuning appointment focused on improving the overall pitch, balance, and consistency of your piano.

02

Pitch Assessment

An evaluation for pianos that have not been tuned recently or may be significantly above or below standard pitch.

03

Performance Preparation

Appointment planning for recitals, recording sessions, lessons, events, and other situations where tuning consistency is especially important.
